Our Vision

Empowering Lives, Enriching Futures

We envision the Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Krishna Alumni Association as a dynamic and inclusive community committed to empowering the lives of its members and enriching the futures of the broader society. We aspire to create a vibrant network that fosters lifelong connections, encourages continuous learning, and contributes positively to the growth and development of our alma mater.

Our Mission

Foster Lifelong Connections

Cultivate a sense of belonging and camaraderie among alumni, promoting enduring connections that transcend time and distance.

Empower Personal and Professional Growth

Provide platforms for mentoring, knowledge-sharing, and collaboration, supporting the personal and professional development of our alumni.

Contribute to JNV Krishna's Excellence

Collaborate with the school administration, faculty, and students to contribute to the continuous improvement and excellence of Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Krishna.

Facilitate Networking and Collaboration

Establish a robust network that facilitates networking, collaboration, and resource-sharing among alumni, fostering opportunities for career advancement and entrepreneurship.

Encourage Social Responsibility

Promote a sense of social responsibility among alumni, encouraging active participation in community service, philanthropy, and social causes for the greater good.

Support Current Students

Extend support to current students through mentorship programs, scholarships, and initiatives that enhance their academic, personal, and extracurricular growth.

Celebrate Achievements

Acknowledge and celebrate the achievements and successes of our alumni, showcasing inspiring stories that serve as a source of motivation for current students and the wider community.

Cultivate a Culture of Continuous Learning

Promote a culture of continuous learning and intellectual curiosity, organizing events, workshops, and discussions that contribute to the intellectual growth of our alumni community.

Enhance Alumni Visibility

Increase the visibility and recognition of JNV Krishna Alumni Association, both within the alumni community and the broader society, showcasing the positive impact and contributions of our members.

Preserve and Promote JNV Krishna's Legacy

Actively contribute to the preservation and promotion of the rich legacy, traditions, and values of Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Krishna, ensuring they are passed down to future generations
