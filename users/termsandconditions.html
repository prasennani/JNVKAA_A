User Agreement JNV Krishna Alumni Association website is a digital platform for the alumni of Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Veleru, Krishna Dist. School. Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas are a system of central schools which are run by Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti, New Delhi, an autonomous organization under the Department of School Education and Literacy, Ministry of Education, Government of India. All the IT services of, and the related data and information at JNV Krishna Alumni Association website are owned, managed and controlled by JNV Krishna Alumni Association, a registered society under the Registrations and Stamps Department, Government of Andhra Pradesh. The digital services at JNV Krishna Alumni Association could be utilized by the alumni for getting help in connecting with the other alumni, organizing alumni meets, getting career guidance, and getting access to the brands owned or managed by other alumni of Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya.
