Launches Innovative Alumni Website for Lifelong Engagement and Professional Growth

Posted On:  30th November 2023

Posted By:  Kranthi Kumar Narne

Proudly announces the launch of its highly anticipated Alumni Website. The platform aims to strengthen connections among graduates, fostering a vibrant network that transcends geographical boundaries and transcends time.

In an ever-evolving professional landscape, the importance of a robust alumni network cannot be overstated. The Alumni Website serves as a digital hub for graduates to reconnect, collaborate, and continue their journey of lifelong learning.

In an era of global mobility, graduates often find themselves dispersed across continents. The Alumni Website serves as a virtual bridge, allowing alumni to stay connected, irrespective of geographical distances.
